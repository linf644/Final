Studio Lighting

September 7, 2020 at 9:00am - 5:00pm
351 North 70th Street Harrisburg, PA 17111 United States


    Class consists of Hands-on exercises to understand the use of light in the studio situation and apply outdoors as well.
    Students will review use of light modifires and lights with varying output capabilities.
    Students will be introduced to placement of studio lights for portraits as well as on location applications.
     Students will practice lighting with multiple lights.
    Students will demonstrate use of techniques in class as an assignment by the end of class.
     Posing positions will be covered to make your portraits more pleasing.
    Students will learn the use of light meters.
    Selection of backgrounds to compliment your portraits for color harmony.
    Course Items: Students should bring a DSLR with corresponding cf/sdf cards. some samples of current work. light meter if owned, if not, one will be available for class exercises. Class notes will be handed out, writing pens, etc . useful to have .Lunch will be provided.
