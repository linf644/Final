About
Professional Photographers of America （PPA） is the world largest non-profit organization that has 30,000 members in more than 50 countries. PPA consists of a group of volunteer councilors, PPA committees, board of directors, and PPA staffs. During the 150-year history, the Professional Photographers of America has taken cares of photographers with dedication. The aim of this organization is to help create a community for professional photographers to exchange their ideas and experience to push them become more creative with their works; and at the same time, to protect the photographers more carefully and help them be more profitable and more successful. There are resources of both online and in-person learning resources and opportunities for the members. Insurance is also provided to the members for protection of the photographers themselves and their business. Certifications are also offered by the PPA to differentiate the photographers among others as well as push their talents to a higher level.
